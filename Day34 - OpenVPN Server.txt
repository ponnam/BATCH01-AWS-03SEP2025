Virtual Private Network - VPN:
------------------------------

--> VPN allows the communication between your network and the Remote Private network.

--> We can access the instances with their public IP address from anywhere with the help of VPN

--> Types of VPN
	
	- Site to Site VPN
	- IP-Sec VPN


--> Site to Site VPN is used to establish the secure communication between 2 different networks.

	ex: Between Branch office and Head office networks or between Your office and AWS VPC 			network

--> IPsec VPN is a type of VPN which allows individual users to access the private network from anywhere across the globe.
